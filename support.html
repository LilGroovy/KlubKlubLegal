Common Questions (FAQ)

Before emailing, you might find your answer here!

  • How do I delete my account?
    You can delete your account by navigating to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. Please note this action is irreversible.
  • How do I report another user?
    You can report a user by visiting their profile, tapping the three-dot menu, and selecting "Report."
